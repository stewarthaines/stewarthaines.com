The photo's from Ulladulla, but for narrative purposes I'm pretending its a north beaches ocean pool at low tide.

27.09.2002

'The' Entrance to Bellevue Hill

meet me down at the jetty landing

V. busy road all the way to Woy Woy (the town so nice they named it twice?) this morning. Stop in Terrigal on the beach front to eat a couple of sweet buns. Breakfast was two bananas and two muesli bars. Pay attention. There will be a test.

To Ettalong just in time to miss a ferry. Good opportunity for a lunch stop though I'm not really hungry. Ferry to Palm Beach. I've heard of that.

Just about to ride off from P.B. when a guy comes up to me and says something about riding. End up talking to Simon (Hi Si) for half an hour. He's heading north and waiting for my ferry to go back. He's got a trailer (bob) and invites me to test ride. Very weird low speed handling. Almost off-putting.

Busy road afternoon to Manly.

Buy ticket and run down the wharf for the ferry (The Ferry) to Circular Quay. Sitting on the top deck watching the landmarks appear around the headlands...

Staying in Bondi with Adam. Proper homely type place. Private bathroom. Lovely.
