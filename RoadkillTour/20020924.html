I saw the sun rise. I got up before the dawn. Tick. I found a different purpose.

24.09.2002

Mungo Brush to Newcastle

the Pidgeon-kicking Principle

Up at 5:30 to catch the sun rise over the dune. Just not early enough to set up the camera before the sun is up.

Watching a pair of hawks (eagles? bird-of-prey.) and a pair of crows dog-fighting for air and treetop supremacy. Screeching and flapping and general carrying-on. Very evenly matched, considered. Crows take to the trees and use guerilla tacticts where the eagles (whatever) are too big, but crows are inferior in the air. Better than TV. Certainly better than 6am TV.

The Pidgeon-kicking Principle  by S.Haines

I kick pidgeons on principle.
Not because I like kicking pidgeons
Not because pidgeons deserve a good kicking
Not because being a pidgeon is wrong
But because there is a principle at stake;
  the pidgeon-kicking principle.

I routinely detain asylum seekers.
Not because I like to detain asylum seekers
Not because asylum seekers deserve to be detained
Not because seeking asylum is wrong
But because there is a principle at stake;
  the pidgeon-kicking principle.

And because I can.

Actually I sometimes enjoy kicking pidgeons.
It's fun.
Try it.
