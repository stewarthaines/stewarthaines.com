10.09.2002

to Byron Bay via Mt Jerusalem N.P.

growth and decay

Dirt roads. Steep. Some walking. Stopped Mullumbimby to recharge (my phone account). Met the local bike shop owner. Chatted chain lube. Bought cycle gloves. Gear freakism roolz. First time on highway proper last 6km into Byron. Pitch tent. Hurry back into town to shop for tea before sun goes down - I turn into a pumpkin at dusk. Nonono. Lights, I mean. I have no lights.

Wonderful feeling to take the luggage off the bike and go for a ride. Legs feel super strong. Super legs. I should have got me a pair of these years ago!
