Scenery to make you feel consumptive.

6.10.2002

past Narooma to Mystery Bay

no such thing as a free festival

whooo. MYSTERY! ADVENTURE! TIREDNESS!

Narooma Blues Festival. Hmm. Looks interesting. I could stand a few hours of live blues from outside the fence. Maybe even pitch my tent close enough to listen all evening.

Maybe not.

No camping for non ticket-holders. Common folk. Have to leave town. Choose council camping ground at Mystery Bay. It costs $17.50 payable at tourist info office at Narooma. It's called the Primitive Camping Ground. I wonder why.

It's primitive. And costs $17.50 for a site for a night. That's too much for a cold shower and pit toilet. But beautiful location, so I don't dwell on it.
