A Blowhole Somewhere.

16.09.2002

to Grassy Head via Macksville

... you should see his hands

Should have stopped in Macksville, but just felt so darned good. And Macksville was nothing special.

Bought cooked breakfast - eggs, bacon, tomato, toast. Just to see how it compares as fuel with porridge. Turn out, not well. No energy in the morning. Not powering up those hills, as expected. (Although I did go on to ride for 5 1/2 hours.

Late morning lunch in Urunga with fellow cyclist Nigel from UK. Travelling north to Townsville. Previously cycled the west coast and various other places.

Very windy, but mostly in my favour so I push on and do a bit of the next morning's ride. Finish up with 92km on the compi. Quite pleased with self.
